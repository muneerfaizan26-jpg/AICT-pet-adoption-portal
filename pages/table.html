Adoption Processing Fees

Our adoption fees are not a purchase price — they help cover essential care costs such as vaccinations, deworming, spaying/neutering, microchipping, and basic health checks. By adopting, you’re supporting our mission to rescue and rehome animals responsibly.

Pet Type Age / Category What’s Included Processing Fee (PKR)
Dog Puppy (under 1 year) Vaccinations, deworming, microchip, spay/neuter 5,000 – 8,000
Dog Adult (1 year and older) Vaccinations, deworming, microchip, spay/neuter 4,000 – 7,000
Cat Kitten or Adult Vaccinations, deworming, microchip, spay/neuter 3,500 – 6,000
Rabbit Any age Health check, basic parasite treatment 2,000 – 4,000
Bird (Parrot, Budgie, etc.) Any age Health check, wing clipping if needed 1,500 – 4,000
Fees may vary slightly depending on the pet’s medical needs and age. We offer partial waivers or sponsorship options for families in financial need — please contact us after submitting an adoption interest form.

Why Pay an Adoption Fee?

Full Veterinary Care

All pets are vaccinated, dewormed, and often spayed/neutered before adoption.

Microchipping

Helps reunite you with your pet if they ever get lost.
