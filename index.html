Pet Care Tips for New Owners

Dogs

Daily exercise, balanced diet, regular vet visits, and positive training are key.

Cats

Provide scratching posts, high perches, interactive toys, and clean litter daily Walt.

Rabbits

Unlimited hay, fresh vegetables, safe chew toys, and spacious housing.

Birds

Large cage, varied diet (seeds, fruits, pellets), daily out-of-cage time, and mental stimulation.

How the Adoption Process Works

  1. Browse & Choose: Explore our pets and find your match.
  2. Show Interest: Click "Adopt Me" on your favorite pet.
  3. Submit Form: Fill out the adoption interest form honestly.
  4. Home Check: We may schedule a quick virtual or in-person visit.
  5. Meet the Pet: Arrange a meeting to see if it's a good fit.
  6. Finalize & Take Home: Pay the processing fee and welcome your new family member!
